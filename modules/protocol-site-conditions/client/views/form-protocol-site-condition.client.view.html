Protocol 1 Site Conditions

Your group is responsible for observations about the land, water and air.

This includes weather, any recent rainfall, tide and current, as well as environmental conditions of the land and the water, pollution, and garbage.

Protocol 2 Oyster Measurements

Your group is responsible for measuring growth and recording mortality of oysters in the main oyster cage.

Protocol 3 Mobile Trap

Your group is responsible for observations about the types and quantities of mobile organisms found within the two compartments of the mobile trap.

One compartment contains mesh and the other contains oyster shells.

Protocol 4 Settlement Tiles

Your group is responsible for recording the sessile organisms growing on each of the four settlement tiles.

Protocol 5 Water Quality

Your group is responsible for collecting water quality samples and measuring water quality parameters.
